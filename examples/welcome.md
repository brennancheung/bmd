# bmd

Dedicated Markdown reader. One window. Sidebar recents. Web rendering.

## Why tables matter

| Column A | Column B | Column C | Column D | Column E |
|----------|----------|----------|----------|----------|
| wide     | enough   | to       | force    | scroll   |
| 1        | 2        | 3        | 4        | 5        |
| alpha    | beta     | gamma    | delta    | epsilon  |

Prose stays in a comfortable column. Tables can use more horizontal space.
